OVERVIEW:

Mankulam and Munnar:

Mankulam and Munnar, both located in Kerala's Idukki district, offer stunning natural beauty and tranquility. Mankulam is a serene, lesser-known village surrounded by lush forests, tea plantations, and waterfalls, ideal for nature walks and wildlife spotting. In contrast, Munnar is a popular hill station renowned for its expansive tea gardens, mist-covered hills, and attractions like the Eravikulam National Park and Anamudi Peak. Both destinations are best visited from October to March, providing a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
