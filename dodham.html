OVERVIEW:

Do Dham Trip:

Embark on a transformative pilgrimage with our Do Dham Yatra, visiting two of the most revered shrines in Hinduism: Kedarnath and Badrinath. Nestled amidst the majestic Himalayas, these sacred sites offer a profound spiritual experience, blending divine blessings with the awe-inspiring beauty of nature.Kedarnath, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple, perched at an altitude of 3,583 meters, is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and the Mandakini River. Pilgrims trek through picturesque trails, experiencing the serenity and spirituality that envelop this ancient shrine.

ITINERY:

Budget Trip plan to Do Dham:


  • Board the train/flight from Chennai to Delhi

  • Arrive in Delhi and transfer to Haridwar via train or road.
  • Check-in at the hotel in Haridwar.
  • Attend the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.
  • Stay in Haridwar

  • Early breakfast and drive to Guptkashi.
  • Check-in at the hotel in Guptkashi.
  • Visit the Ardhnarishwar Temple.
  • Stay in Guptkashi.

  • Drive to Sonprayag, then take a local jeep to Gaurikund. Begin the trek to Kedarnath (14 km).
  • Reach Kedarnath and check-in at the guesthouse
  • Visit Kedarnath Temple for evening Aarti.
  • Stay in Kedarnath.

  • Attend the early morning Aarti at Kedarnath Temple.
  • Trek back to Gaurikund and then drive to Guptkashi.
  • Relax at the hotel.
  • Stay in Guptkashi.

  • Early breakfast and drive to Badrinath via Joshimath
  • Check-in at the hotel in Badrinath
  • Visit Badrinath Temple for evening Aarti.
  • Stay in Badrinath.

  • Visit Badrinath Temple for early morning Aarti
  • Explore Mana Village, the last Indian village before the Tibet border
  • Drive to Rudraprayag and check-in at the hotel.
  • Stay in Rudraprayag.

  • Drive to Rishikesh.
  • Check-in at the hotel in Rishikesh
  • Visit Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, and attend the Ganga Aarti at Parmarth Niketan.
  • Stay in Rishikesh.

  • Drive to Delhi.
  • Arrive in Delhi and transfer to the hotel
  • Free time for shopping or sightseeing
  • Stay in Delhi.

  • Transfer to the airport/railway station for the journey back to Chennai.

  • Arrive in Chennai, concluding the Do Dham Yatra.
