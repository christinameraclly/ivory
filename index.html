KEDARNATH
Kedarnath is a sacred town in Uttarakhand, India, renowned for the Kedarnath Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, set against the stunning backdrop of the Himalayas.
GOA
Goa is a small coastal state in India known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese-influenced architecture.
KOLUKKUMALAI
Kolukkumalai is a picturesque hill station in Tamil Nadu, India, famous for its sprawling tea plantations and being home to one of the highest tea estates in the world.
MANALI
Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and as a gateway to the Himalayas.
KUDREMUKHA
Kudremukha is a mountain range and a national park in Karnataka, India, known for its lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and the distinct horse-face shape of its peak.
MANKULAM
Mankulam is a serene village in Kerala, India, known for its lush forests, picturesque landscapes, and proximity to wildlife sanctuaries.
