OVERVIEW:

Wayanad:

Wayanad is a picturesque district in the northeastern part of Kerala, India, renowned for its lush greenery, mist-covered hills, and rich biodiversity. Nestled in the Western Ghats, Wayanad is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The region is known for its expansive spice plantations, tea gardens, and wildlife sanctuaries, such as the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, which is home to elephants, leopards, and various bird species.
