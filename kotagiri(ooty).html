OVERVIEW:

Kotagiri(ooty):

Kotagiri is a charming hill station located in the Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu, India. Nestled at an altitude of around 1,793 meters (5,883 feet), it is one of the oldest and most scenic hill stations in the region. Kotagiri is known for its pleasant climate, lush tea estates, and tranquil ambiance, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat. The area offers various attractions, including Catherine Falls, Elk Falls, and the scenic Kodanad View Point, which provides stunning vistas of the surrounding valleys and mountains. Kotagiri's rich biodiversity and serene environment make it a popular spot for trekking, bird watching, and exploring the natural beauty of the Nilgiris. The best time to visit is from October to May when the weather is cool and conducive to outdoor activities.
