OVERVIEW:

Kullu Manali:

Manali is a picturesque hill station located in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, India, at an altitude of about 2,050 meters (6,726 feet). Nestled in the northern Himalayas, Manali is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, including snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and pristine rivers. It serves as a popular year-round destination for both tourists and adventure enthusiasts.
