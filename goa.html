OVERVIEW:

Goa Trip:

Goa, India's smallest state, packs a punch with its unique blend of sun, sand, and culture. Nestled on the southwestern coast, Goa is renowned for its beautiful beaches like Baga, Calangute, and Palolem, where tourists unwind amidst palm-fringed shores and vibrant beach shacks offering seafood delights. Beyond beaches, Goa's charm lies in its rich Portuguese heritage reflected in colonial architecture, whitewashed churches, and bustling markets like Mapusa and Anjuna. Adventurers flock to Dudhsagar Waterfalls for trekking, while wildlife enthusiasts explore the mangrove forests of the Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary.
