OVERVIEW:

Thailand Trip:

Thailand or officially known as the Kingdom of Thailand is one of the most visited countries in Southeast Asia. It may be surrounded by equally beautiful countries like Myanmar (northwest), Laos (northeast), Cambodia (southeast) and Malaysia (south), but it has distinct treasures to share among travelers and tourists.Thailand also has beautiful temples and botanical gardens, like Suan Nong Nooch in Pattaya. Thailand is also known for its beautiful pink lily lakes. They bloom on Red Lotus Lake between November and February.
