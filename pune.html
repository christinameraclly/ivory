OVERVIEW:

Pune Trip:

Pune is a vibrant city located in the western Indian state of Maharashtra. Known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance, Pune is often referred to as the "Oxford of the East" due to its numerous educational institutions and universities. The city is a major IT hub, attracting professionals from across the country. Pune's cultural scene is lively, with numerous festivals, music concerts, and theater performances taking place throughout the year. Key attractions include the historic Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and the serene Osho Ashram. The city's pleasant climate, with cool winters and moderate monsoons, makes it a desirable destination year-round. Additionally, Pune serves as a gateway to nearby hill stations like Lonavala and Khandala, offering scenic getaways from urban life.
